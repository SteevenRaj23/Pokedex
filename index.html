Pokédex: Unveiling the Wonders of the Unseen

The Pokédex keeps track of the Pokémon you've seen and caught. Use the Pokédex to learn more about the different species of Pokémon and their Evolutions. The Pokédex is a fictional device within the Pokémon world that serves as an encyclopedia of Pokémon species, providing information about each Pokémon.
